Protector Game

In the year 2399, when Earth was rendered unhabitable by climate change, the ship Prometheus was humanity’s final hope. Built jointly by NASA and SpaceX, docked at the andromeda galaxy, it is home to 50K people.

The separatist rebel group led by terrorist leader, however, is determined to take the ship by force. When the army of thirty thousand ships showed up on Prometheus’s radar, it is clear that the ship will not stand a chance.

The president had ordered an evacuation by ejecting delta-sleep life pods in the hope that the nearby colony Odyssey can pick up some survivor. But evacuation takes time. The decommissioned battle-class destroyer, Kerberos is the only hope for buying that time.

Hop onboard the Kerberos and fight the good fight! Brave souls, do not go gentle into that good night!
